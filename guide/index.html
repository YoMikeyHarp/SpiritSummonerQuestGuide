The Great Land Level 25▼

Open plains unlocking after King's Heart Forest. Higher-level encounters with improved XP and gold rewards across repeatable tasks.

Sky Tower Level 30▼

Vertical ascent zone. Lv.30 required. Strong spirit encounters as you climb higher.

The Dragon's Lair Level 32▼

Unlocks from a task in Basalon Town (available after clearing Dark Shire). Fire and dragon-type territory.

Wall of Gaia Level 32▼

Parallel to Dragon's Lair at Lv.32. Ground and earth-type encounters.
